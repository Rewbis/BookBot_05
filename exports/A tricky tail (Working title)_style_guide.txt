STYLE GUIDE: A tricky tail (Working title)
========================================
[TONE]: Gritty, realistic, brutal at times, but frequently holding up some fragment of the noble nature people are capable of.
[VOICE]: First person, switching between the perspective of the antagonist, Gonad Goldrump, with other chapters from the perspective of his enemy of the day.
[VOCABULARY]: Keep it concise, don't indulge in puffery - if a fact or event is mundane, feel free to describe it as such.
[GLOBAL_POV]: First Person
[TENSE]: Past
