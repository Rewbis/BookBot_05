WORLD INFO: A tricky tail (Working title)
========================================
[SETTING]: A large country, young by other standards, bridging a continent from east to west. Encompassing vast plains, mountain ranges, deserts, mighty forests... A powerful alliance of city states making the "united dominions" much like the united states, but low tech (medieval) and low magic (game of thrones)
[HISTORY]: the united dominions have grown complacent and forgotten the principles they fought for. it was not their fault, their forebears were so successful in overcoming evils, they raised a generation who never knew of them. And that generation raised a new generation, who had no way to remember the grifts and tricks possible - they thought the old ways were the best as they had never experienced the horrors of e.g. poor dental care, low hygiene, corruption at every level, favouritism, cronyism, foreign interests actively seeking their downfall... they thought of them as issues other countries had, not them, never them... An age of prosperity, with a worm eating, growing in the centre. They had fought several wars with low impact. They were proud and thought it justified.

[RULES]: Each city state has some autonomy, but rules under the central government, with representation. Magic is extremely rare, magical creatures are extrene
[OTHER]: Perverts and cheats have always existed, but here they have found how to go undetected for too long. One after another, they are exposed and brought to light, but others are still operating in the shadows, learning and improving their camouflage.
